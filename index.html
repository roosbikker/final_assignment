Music is essential in a child's development. That is why we give every child in the Netherlands access to music education, from singing to DJing and from drumming to playing the piano and guitar; 'Music at School' is a broad educational program provided by qualified teachers. This program covers almost all instruments so that a child can develop his or her musical interests broadly. This focuses on 'learning together', whereby the child learns to make music together. We provide weekly lessons as well as occasional workshops at primary schools across the country.
