HOME PAGE

The home page is the welcome page of the website. At the top of this page, like every page of Breda Tunes, you can see the logo on the links, the navigation bar and the search option. Underneath, the jumbotron is visible, which draws the visitor's attention through a screaming headline with bold letters. This headline makes the visitors want to see more. Then there is a bar of text that gives an introduction and introduces the school to the visitor. Below that is a carousel of photos that give an impression of the school. Then there is also a piece of text that provides further information about the school. At the bottom of the page you can find the footer with the name of the school, the telephone number, copyright and the free trial button.

Our mission is to give every child in the Netherlands access to the best music education in primary, secondary, special education and after-school care. Through our 'Music at School' program, which is taught by qualified teachers and also provides support for the teacher, the child not only learns everything about music, but also learns to play the popular instruments. From singing to DJing and from drumming to playing the piano and guitar; 'Music at School' is a broad educational program provided by qualified teachers. This program covers almost all instruments so that a child can develop his or her musical interests broadly. This focuses on 'learning together', whereby the child learns to make music together. We provide weekly lessons as well as occasional workshops at primary schools across the country.
