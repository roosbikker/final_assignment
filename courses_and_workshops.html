FREE TRIAL COURSES & WORKSHOPS

At the Tilburg Music School you will only be taught by the best qualified teachers. Teachers who, with their many years of theoretical and practical knowledge and experiences, can assist you in your musical development as optimally as possible. With a lesson program adapted to your musical wishes, the Vocal course in Tilburg is the right place for the development of your voice. Our courses and workshops are unique as we adapt to your wishes. This ensures that everyone gets the right and appropriate attention.

No dusty teaching methods, but rather a teaching program adapted to your musical wishes. You not only learn your favorite songs, but also the techniques and methods behind these songs. In this way you develop your voice very quickly, and you can achieve your musical goals as quickly as possible. A lot of attention is also paid to the performance, so you learn how to perform on a stage and you learn to overcome any stage fright.

DJ

Do you dream of getting the audience wild with your music and mixes on a big stage? Or maybe you even have some experience playing at parties? Then this DJ course is for you!

GUITAR

A guitar is a stringed instrument and can be used acoustically or electrically. The strings are struck by the fingers or a plectrum. The instrument is used in all kinds of styles (from classical to metal rock).

PIANO

In this practical course for pianists and other keyboardists with experience, you develop your feeling of testing so that you can play more easily in all keys. Chord exercises make your fingers feel more and more at home on the entire keyboard.

VOCALS

A course to discover your voice and (further) develop your voice technique! All basic elements of singing are covered: breathing, use of the voice, articulation, intonation and exercises to improve your rhythm. Of course we will also sing, so that you can immediately apply what you have learned.
